## What It Does

AURA Eyes is a retinal vascular screening platform designed to support early clinical review, streamline patient workflows, and help medical teams move from image capture to decision more efficiently.

The system is built around three outcomes:

- accelerate retinal screening with AI-assisted analysis
- reduce friction between patients, clinics, and doctors
- keep medical workflows auditable, role-based, and secure

---

## Key Capabilities

### Clinical Screening
- retinal image upload and review
- AI-assisted screening output with heatmaps and explanatory results
- final diagnosis confirmation by ophthalmologists

### Patient Journey
- OTP and Google-based account access
- appointment booking and consultation requests
- screening result tracking and follow-up support

### Professional Workflow
- ophthalmologist credential verification
- availability management and case review
- secure chat and collaboration for consultation flows

### Organization and Admin Control
- organization account approval
- billing and report handling
- revenue sharing and operational oversight
